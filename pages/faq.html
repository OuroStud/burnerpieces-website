Frequently Asked Questions

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days within India. Metro cities typically receive orders within 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available at checkout for 2-3 day delivery.

We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached. Initiate a return through our contact page or email support@burnerpieces.com.

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS from Shiprocket. You can also track your order on the Shiprocket tracking page using your AWB number.

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and wallets through our secure Razorpay payment gateway.

Currently, we ship only within India. International shipping will be available soon. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated!

Each product page includes a detailed size chart. If you're between sizes, we recommend going with the larger size for a comfortable fit. Contact us for personalized sizing advice.

Yes! Size exchanges are free within 7 days of delivery. Simply contact us with your order number and the size you need, and we'll arrange the exchange.
