Store Policy

Customer Care

At Burner Pieces, customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. Our dedicated support team is available Monday through Saturday, 10AM to 7PM IST, to assist you with any queries about orders, sizing, returns, or general questions.

Reach us at support@burnerpieces.com or call +91 98765 43210. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

Privacy & Safety

We take your privacy seriously. All personal information collected during the checkout process is encrypted and stored securely. We use Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway, ensuring your financial data is never stored on our servers.

We collect only the information necessary to process your order and improve your shopping experience. Your data is never sold to third parties. For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Wholesale Inquiries

Interested in stocking Burner Pieces in your retail store? We welcome wholesale partnerships and offer competitive pricing for bulk orders. Our minimum order quantity starts at 50 pieces per style.

For wholesale pricing, catalogues, and partnership information, please contact us at wholesale@burnerpieces.com with your business details and requirements.

Payment Methods

We accept a wide range of payment options through our secure Razorpay integration: Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Rupay), Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Net Banking (all major banks), and popular wallets.
